Film & Animation Collaborations

Journey Through Creativity

An animated short by Tevy Dubray.

A light piano-based score with orchestral flourishes, reminiscent of Erik Satie. The texture—with introduction of new instruments—is made to mirror the protagonist's own artistic realization throughout her life. (Added my own music to this as a pre-existing short.)

Sad Movie, the Movie

An experimental short by Arius Ziaee and Siyuan Liu, from the DIY Visual Effects class.

Its music is rhythmic and highly synthesized, embracing its otherworldly atmosphere. As the music falls into calamity and distortion, you gleam a cryptic story of intergalactic love and haunting tragedy. (And maybe a bit of comedy.)

Morning Glory (Abridged)

A 20-minute film directed by Cynthia Chatman.

Features a classical orchestral soundtrack that grows increasingly ominous as the characters discover the dark truth behind the Morning Glory Choir. References to Handel's Messiah can be heard as a recurring motif.

Scale of the Sea

An animated short by Sean Akane-Kishiro.

Accompanied by a light-orchestral waltz that evokes the distant surreality of the coastline. The harmony carefully toes the boundary between the familiar and the unknown, playing into the simultaneously cute and sinister atmosphere of this uniquely crafted world.
